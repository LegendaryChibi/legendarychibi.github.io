Platform: PC 
Engine: Unity 3D 
Production Time: 12 Weeks 
Size of Team: 9 
Role: Lead Programmer

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Custom Minigame System Development: Designed and implemented a modular system supporting up to nine unique minigames, complete with integrated stat tracking and gameplay management systems.
  • Agile Project Management: Facilitated sprints and collaborated with other leads to plan and achieve milestone goals, ensuring consistent progress and alignment with project deadlines.
  • Cross-Disciplinary Collaboration: Partnered with programmers, designers, and artists to integrate diverse creative elements into cohesive gameplay.
    UI, VFX, and Animation Support: Contributed to implementing user interfaces, visual effects, and animations, enhancing the player experience.
  • Codebase Maintenance: Played a key role in building and maintaining a robust, scalable codebase, supporting the project’s growth and adaptability.

Overview

REVEREX is an award-winning asymmetrical two-player co-op game in which players don the roles of Lab Technicians operating an experimental device designed to bring patients out of a coma. Featuring 3 Unique Levels & Endings, Unusual Gravity Mechanics, 2-player asymmetric action, and cutting-edge graphics. 

Roles:

Navigator: Responsible for navigating the trippy and unusual dream world of the coma patient. Run, jump, and parkour your way through the dream world! 

Vitalist: Completes minigames designed to maintain the vital stats and health of the patient’s physical body. The outcome of these minigames directly impacts the performance of the Navigator.
