Overview

The scientists at the Zlyodomov Biomedical Manufacture Concern (ZBMC) think they’ve created a device which can pull people out of comas: the REVEREX. 

As their top technicians, its up to you and a friend to operate the machine through it’s maiden voyage, ensuring the stability of your patient’s mind and body through the entire ordeal. Navigate stages rife with unusual geometry, shifting gravity, and strange visuals. 

Complete challenging maintenance tasks to make traversal easier. Get out again before the dream world, or your patient, are destabilized for good.

Roles:

Navigator: The Navigator is responsible for navigating the trippy and unusual dream world of the coma patient. Run, Jump, and Parkour your way through the dream world!

Vitalist: The Vitalist must complete minigames designed to maintain the vital stats and health of the patient’s physical body. The outcome of these minigames directly impacts the performance of the Navigator.

Please note that REVEREX: DX contains flashing lights, bright colors and other visuals that may cause adverse effects to individuals with photosensitivity issues including Epilepsy. Exercise caution before playing if you have a history of photosensitivity.
