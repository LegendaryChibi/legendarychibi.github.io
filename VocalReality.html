Role: Blueprints  Programmer

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Designed and implemented a subtitle system synchronized with video playback, ensuring accurate text display during standard, fast-forward, and reverse playback modes.
  • Developed a screen fade functionality that allows players to gradually fade the screen to black, offering an optional tool for players to manage visual or emotional overwhelm during gameplay.
  • Created a player-friendly mode (Stop and Breathe) enabling users to temporarily suspend gameplay by fully fading the screen, providing a calm, controlled environment before resuming the game.

Overview

Vocal Reality is a groundbreaking VR experience designed to help individuals with selective mutism and social anxiety build confidence in everyday social situations. 

With its immersive 360-degree videos, Vocal Reality places users in realistic scenarios—like visiting the dentist, taking a driving test, or engaging in conversations at the store—making it easier to face real-world challenges in a safe and controlled virtual environment. Each scenario includes subtitles with guided prompts to help users respond naturally and participate in meaningful dialogue.
