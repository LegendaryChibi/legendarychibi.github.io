Overview

Darwin's Diner is a first-of-its-kind, dog-based, PB&J-like game. In this game, you are a dog and you make PB&Js for your customers. And really... that's all you can do. Dogs don't have hands like humans!

Spread, Swipe and Satisfy your customers with your impeccable Sandwich Skills!
